Privacy Policy for Notify-and-Taskify

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Notify-and-Taskify is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share any personal information when you use our application.

2. No Data Collection

We collect absolutely no personal data. The App is designed to function completely offline, and we have intentionally built it without any data collection mechanisms.

Specifically, we do NOT:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track your location or device information
  • Monitor your usage or app interactions
  • Access your photos, contacts, or other personal data
  • Use analytics or crash reporting tools
  • Display advertisements or use advertising IDs

3. Local-Only Storage

All data created within the App (notes, tasks, reminders) is stored exclusively on your device using local SQLite database storage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No data is transmitted to any server or cloud service
  • No data backups are made to external services
  • Your data remains private and under your control

4. Permissions

The App does NOT request or require access to: Camera, Microphone, Location, Contacts, Storage (except internal app storage), or Phone state.

5. No Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud backup services
  • No payment processors

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages as it does not collect any personal information. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13, as no data collection occurs.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security measures
  • No data transmission occurs, eliminating interception risks
  • You maintain complete control over your data

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the App
  • Deletion: Delete individual notes/tasks or uninstall the App to remove all data
  • Export: You can copy content from the App manually
  • Correction: Edit your notes and tasks directly within the App

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date. We will notify users of significant changes through the Google Play Store listing.
